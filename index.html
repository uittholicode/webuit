About Our College

Located in the serene village of Tholicode(Pulimoodu) in Tholicode Panchayath, Our college is a center of excelence dedicated to providing quality education in a peacefull and nature rich environment. Just 13 kilometeres from Nedumangad town, our campus offers students a calm and inspiring atmosphere for academic and personal growth.

Our college is one of the 34 learning centers governed by the University of Kerala(UOK),Which established these institutions 25 years ago to promote technology-focused education in rural areas. These centers offers specialized undergraduate programs such as BBA, B.Sc Computer Science, Electronics, BCA and BCom. Commerce with Computer Applications,ensuring that students from remote locations have access to modern education and career opputunities.
